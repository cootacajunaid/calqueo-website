A calculator designed to be beautiful.

Calqueo does four things — add, subtract, multiply, divide — and does them gracefully. As you type, your expression is spelled out in words: twelve plus seven times three. A quiet sentence that makes you read your math, not just see it.

It follows true order of operations, the way mathematics intends. And when you press equals, the answer takes the stage while your expression stays beneath it — in figures and in words.
