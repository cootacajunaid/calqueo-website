A calculator designed to be beautiful.

What makes Calqueo unlike any other calculator is language. As you type, your expression is spelled out in words — twelve plus seven times three. Decimals, negatives, and long chains of operations all read naturally, updating live with every key.

Calqueo follows true order of operations: multiplication and division resolve before addition and subtraction.

The design is a single deep green with one gold accent and a serif made for numbers. Soft haptics on every key.
